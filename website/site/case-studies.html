Case Studies

Examples of measurable improvements from transparent, consent-based agent oversight.

Research Ops Team | 38% Faster Root-Cause Analysis

A research team instrumented local agent runs and connected memory revisions to task outcomes. Theia surfaced stale-memory dependencies and contradictory assumptions before production handoff.

  • Primary gains: faster diagnosis, fewer reruns
  • Most used view: workflow timeline + reasoning alerts

Platform Team | Better Workflow Reliability

A platform group added tool-output mismatch checks and loop alerts across automation-heavy flows. Operators used alert evidence links to enforce safer handoff thresholds.

  • Primary gains: reduced silent workflow failures
  • Most used view: cross-agent comparison and checkpoints

Governance Stakeholders | Audit Readiness Improvements

A governance team required explicit connector scopes and approval traceability. Theia’s audit timeline reduced ambiguity during internal controls review.

  • Primary gains: clearer accountability and evidence packaging
  • Most used view: permissions + audit lineage export
